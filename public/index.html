ABOUT MUSIC THERAPY

Music therapy is an evidence-based practice that addresses emotional, social, cognitive, physical, and spiritual needs. Services are offered to individuals of all ages and abilities by a board-certified music therapist.

OUR SERVICES

We offer music therapy that encourages healthy, dynamic relationships with self and others. We value open communication, respect, humor, and trust. These qualities are supported in our groups and demonstrated in our co-therapeutic relationship. We use an eclectic approach, with emphasis on person-centered and humanistic care to foster growth and achieve goals based on individual needs.
